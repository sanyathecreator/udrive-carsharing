FAQ

Answering Your Questions

We have made the conditions for using our carsharing service as simple as possible. If you still have any questions, please contact us in any convenient way.

You can book a car through our mobile app. Simply select your desired vehicle, choose the pickup location, and confirm your booking.

To use our carsharing service, you must be at least 21 years old, have a valid driver's license, and a credit or debit card for payment.

Payment is processed automatically through the app using the credit or debit card linked to your account. You will be charged based on the duration and distance of your ride.

In case of an accident, ensure everyone's safety first. Then, contact our support team immediately through the app or call our emergency number provided in the app.
