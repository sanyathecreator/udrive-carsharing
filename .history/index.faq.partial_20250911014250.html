FAQ

Answering Your Questions

We have made the conditions for using our carsharing service as simple as possible. If you still have any questions, please contact us in any convenient way.

What cars can I rent?
You can book cars of any class for short-term rent, from compact city coupes to minivans.
What if I need extra time?
When booking a car, you specify the duration of your rental. If you need some extra time, a special tariff will be applied.
Can I increase the mileage limit?
Yes, in this case, select the appropriate option when booking a car.
Are the cars insured?
Yes, we provide secondary insurance for all trips.
Where can I download the UDRIVE app?
The UDRIVE carsharing app is available for iOS and Android. You can download it from Google Play and App Store.
What cities do you cover?
We cover the majority of US cities, including New York, Washington, Miami, Boston, etc.
How much does carsharing cost?
We have different tariffs based on the class of the car you select and on the trip length. The minimum cost is $1 per minute.
